British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is a sturdy and calm breed with a stocky build, plush coat, and a charming round face that resembles a teddy bear. Known for its easygoing and affectionate personality, this cat forms strong bonds with its families while enjoying both quiet time and playful bursts. Their adaptability makes them well-suited for indoor living, thriving in various home environments.

Ragdoll Cat

The Ragdoll cat is known for its relaxed nature and tendency to go limp when held. Its striking blue eyes and soft, semi-long fur make it a cuddly and affectionate companion.
